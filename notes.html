IB HL Math Analysis and Approaches

v4.0

Here are my notes for the IB HL Math Analysis and Approaches curriculum, updated to the 2021 exam year.

I tried to cover every single thing in the course, but tend to be more thorough in explaining the harder topics like vectors or probability, compared to algebra or geometry.

These notes could also be used for the SL curriculum, just ignoring the topics that aren't covered.

PS: Unlike the Computer Science notes, these notes should be used more as a revision resource, rather than learning resource, since the notes recap everything, but don't really prove or explain in depth.
